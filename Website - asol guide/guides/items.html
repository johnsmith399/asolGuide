ITEMS

Starting Items

Corrupting potion: Buy this if you are in a heavy poke matchup (ex. Syndra, Viktor, Azir).

Tear and 1/2 potions: Ideal start. Buy this if poke isn’t as bad to start scaling sooner.

Doran’s ring 2 potions: Buy this into champions that want to jump on you early (ex. Sylas, Fizz). The health is good to stay alive, and the AP helps in trading back.

Early Buys

Tear: If you didn’t start tear.

Refillable: Good into poke matchups, don’t buy if not getting poked down.

Dark Seal: It’s not the best because you don’t have damage early. Don’t int your buy for lost chapter/catalyst. Can buy it later.

Boots: If you didn’t take magic boots vs skill shot matchups.

Fiendish Codex: If you are going Liandry's and don’t have enough for lost chapter. It’s a good buy because the ability haste lets you stack faster with your e.

Lost Chapter: Good item if building into Liandry's. The mana helps a lot with stacking stardust with e.

Catalyst of Aeons: Very good for laning. Lot's of sustain, lots of mana.

Blasting wand: It is okay if you are building ROA, but if you are building ROA you probably need Catalyst first.

Boots

Sorcerer’s Shoes: Best on Asol.

Mercury Treads: Good vs double ap mid and jg. Can also just sit on null-magic mantle if they don’t have much cc.

Ionian boots of lucidity: Not the best. Flash cooldown is not that important, and ability haste isn’t the best on asol. Can buy if you base on bad gold.

Core Items

Liandry's Anguish: This mythic allows you to spike earlier, subsequently allowing you to stack stardust earlier because you have more AP. Also does more damage overall. However, this is a very greedy mythic. Without catalyst for lane, it can punish you extremely hard in the early game. You also have less health to work with as opposed to going ROA, so you are much more reliant on your damage. Takes a lot more skill, because you need to know when you can go in.

Rod of Ages: This mythic is good for new people. It also provides very good scaling and sustain.

Rylai's Crystal Scepter: Not a mythic but you can go this item first if you are planning to go Liandy’s. It allows you to get hp early without going for ROA rush. This is an ok item to go first if you are against a melee champ that you need sustain early against and the Rylai’s slow is valuable vs. them. Build this second if you did not build it first.

Archangels: Go this third. It gives lots of survivability and ability haste.

Rabadon’s Death Cap: Go this fourth if they are not stacking mr. You want to buy this as soon as possible to get a huge spike.

Situational Items

Mejai’s Soulstealer: It’s a decent item since asol is ranged, but be careful about dying and losing all your stacks. Champs like vi can just jump on you and you are dead.

Oblivion Orb Not the best because you do not want to be delaying your core items. However, you do have lots of aoe damage to proc the grievous wounds.

Void Staff: Buy this 4th if they are stacking mr, maybe 3rd if they are stacking mr earlier.

Morellonomicon: IYou get good stats, but there isn’t much point upgrading oblivion orb and delaying more important items.

Zhonya’s/Banshee’s Go this 3rd or 4th if you NEED to buy it or are unable to play the game. Delaying Rabadon’s by an entire item is a massive dps loss. Zhonya’s is good vs. assassins, and Banshee’s is good vs champs with long range cc (ex. Thresh, Blitz, Lux, Morgana, etc.)

Lich Bane You could go this item 50 minutes in the game after selling boots for 6th item, as it provides movement speed, ap, and ability haste.
