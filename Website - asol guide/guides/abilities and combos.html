ABILITIES

Passive

You get stacks of stardust from abilities. More stardust = more win.

Q: Breath of Light

Big laser. Each full second this laser is on an enemy they get dealt extra damage + % max health damage that scales on # of stardust and give you a stack of stardust.

W: Astral Flight

Big flight. During the flight, your Q has no cooldown and does more damage. You also get unobstructed vision (you can see everything on your screen except for bushes).

E: Singularity

Big black hole. Black hole does damage and executes enemies in the center that scales with stardust. If enemies die while in the black hole you get stardust (5 for champs, 3 for large minions/monsters, 1 for small minions/monsters). You also get 1 stardust for each second a champ stands in it. Prep minions and E them all like you would a veigar Q.

R: Falling Star

Big meteorite strike. Stuns champions for 1 second and gives 5 stardust per champion hit. Every 75 stardust stacked without having The Skies Descend upgrades your R to The Skies Descend, giving a bigger meteorite strike, more damage, and knocking up enemies instead of just stunning them. The Skies Descend will also send out a shockwave that slows and reveals all enemies.



COMBOS

E -> Q

When you need to clear minion waves.

E -> R

Slow them with E then bonk them with R.
