RUNES

First Strike primary runes

Good into free matchup, melee matchup, or vs a champ you can’t poke down anyway. Allows for great scaling.

Magical footwear, future’s market / minion demat, cosmic insight.

Go future’s market when you really need an early item like catalyst. Go minion demat for more wave control and easier stacking.

Unsealed Spellbook primary runes

Good into assassin matchup (ex. Leblanc, Zed, Fizz). Allows you to swap to defensive summoners, making laning against them a lot easier.

Magical footwear / perfect timing, future’s market, cosmic insight.

Can go perfect timing vs ap assassin to be able to rush mercs, but magical footwear is still very good. Future’s market allows you to get catalyst earlier, which makes you a lot tankier.

Arcane Comet primary runes

Go vs. champs that outrange you, destroy you in lane too hard that you can’t run first strike, and champs that don’t have a lot of sustain. Ideal vs. longer range mages or difficult mage matchups (ex. Syndra, Zoe, Ori).

Manaflow band, transcendence, gathering storm / scorch.

Can go scorch if they are a champ that does not have sustain, doesn’t run tp, gets corrupting pot, etc. Overall, gathering storm is better.

SUMMONER SPELLS

Flash

Go flash. Good defensive spell.

Teleport

Tp is good because you are a weak laner and you are a good side laner.

Exhaust

Exhaust can be good vs. an assassin and you can swap to tp at level 6 or other summoner spells to help you.

Cleanse

You could go cleanse if you are against a lot of cc (ex. Lissandra, Leblanc). It would be better to go Mercury Treads, but if you are going magic boots then cleanse is ok.
